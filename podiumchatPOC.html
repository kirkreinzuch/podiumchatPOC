Podium Chat POC Test

1. In order to have chat available, an agent must be online in Salesforce and assigned to a queue within Omni-Channel Supervisor. Additionally, assigning skills will allow your reps to take Chats for certain customer tiers, and Products.

2. Start a chat, enter in contact details (use one that already exists in SF with a tier) for it to route correctly. An agent in SF should have that skill and available to accept the chat, or you will see the chat waiting in the Skills Backlog.

3. Chat should open, to person with correct skills. Chat should relate to the contact in Salesforce (If no contact is in Salesforce, no contact will be assigned) and a case will be created/related to the Chat.

4. Work the chat, close the chat, then close the related case or follow up with them via email.
